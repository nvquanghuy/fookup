About FookUp

FookUp is the first-of-its-kind cooking contest for Vietnamese working professional and students in Singapore. With this initiative, we aim to create an excellent playground for people to connect, learn about Vietnamese cuisine and most important of all, have real fun through cooking.

Competition Format:

Round 1 - Prelim: Online Puzzle-solving contest

Round 2 - Semi Finals: Top 8 short-listed teams will participate in on-site cooking challenges, tasty quizzes and small cook-off event.

Round 3 - Finals: Top 4 teams will compete in a final cook-off challenge
