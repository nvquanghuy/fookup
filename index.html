About FookUp

FookUp is the first-of-its-kind cooking contest for Vietnamese community in Singapore. Participants of the event will have chances to indulge in the Vietnam cuisine world while enjoying the cooking fun and getting to know more new friends.

The contest is open to all Vietnamese people. Participants will join in teams of three people.

Competition Format

FookUp 2014 will consist of 3 rounds: online game marathon, semi-final and finale. Both semi-final and finale will take place in 1 full day. 4 teams from the semi-finalists will be selected in the morning to proceed to the finale in the afternoon.

Round 0 - Registration: 24/3 (Monday) – 5/4 (Sunday)

  • Registration closes at 23:59 on Sunday, 5 April 2014. Participants will join in teams of three (3) strictly
  • By registering for the FookUp 2014 Competition, participants indicate that they are available for all the dates listed in the below schedule
  • Team composition and eligibility:
    • The competition is open to all Vietnamese without any limitation to age, gender, education or occupation, etc. All that participants need is a creative and open mind to take up the fun as well as the challenges, to make friends and to “go art” with the food ;)
    • Participants are not required to be excellent in cooking techniques. It would be sufficient for participants to be equipped with the basic cooking skills such as boiling egg or cooking rice. It is the utmost importance that participants possess CREATIVITY and PASSION in food :)
    • Should there are changes to the team members, teams must notify the organizers early to assess on a case-by-case basis

Round 1 - Game Marathon: 12/4 (Saturday) - 20/4 (Sunday)

  • Game Marathon closes at 23:59 on Sunday, 19 April 2014
  • Game Marathon comprises of 5 sub-rounds, each sub-round to take place every 2 days. The exact timing the sub-round to commence will be announced 1 day in advance on FookUp 2014 Facebook Fanpage (for example, if the sub-round starts at 9pm on Monday 14 April, the organizers will announce so on Sunday). So stay tuned and keep refreshing our Facebook page for the latest updates :)
  • Game Marathon is an online challenge competing for speed and accuracy
    • For each sub-round, a food-related quiz consisting of 1-2 questions will be released to each team via email
    • Teams will have 2 hours from the releasing time to work on the quiz and get back to the organizers via email
    • The results will be evaluated based on speed and accuracy. Within 2 hours from the releasing time, the teams delivering the most accurate answers in the shortest time will get full marks. Time is considered as the time the organizers receive the answers
    • Teams are able to make changes to the answers they have already submitted. But beware that every change will be at the teams’ expense of time
    • Teams will be notified by email that their work has been received. If teams do not receive such email by 30 minutes after their submission, please promptly notify the organisers
    • Late submission after 2 hours from the releasing time will not be entertained
    • After each sub-round, organizers will announce a dashboard of the top 10 teams with the highest scores. These rankings will be counted accumulatively and will vary after different sub-rounds, depending on the teams’ performances
    • Up to 8 teams will be selected to advance to the semi-final round

Round 2 & 3 - Semi-final and Finale: 26/4 (Saturday)

  • The Semi-final round will take place from 8.30am – 12pm Saturday 26 April 2014 to select the best 4 teams
  • The top 4 teams will proceed to the Finale in the same afternoon from 2 – 6pm of the same day
  • Both Semi-final round and Finale consists of 2 – 3 fun challenges and a main cook-off
    • Winners are decided by the accumulated points of all the activities involved
    • The main cook-off is weighted as 60% of the total points
    • All the ingredients as well as cooking utensils will be prepared by organizers to ensure fairness among different teams
    • Venue of the semi-final and finale will be announced soon on FookUp Facebook Fanpage and Website, so stay tuned!!!
